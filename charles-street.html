About the Property

A Piece of Old
Belfast

Built in the early 1800s, this Victorian townhouse sits in a quiet cul-de-sac off Great Victoria Street — tucked away from the buzz yet just a 2 minute walk to Belfast city centre. It’s one of the biggest draws of this property.

Recently fully refurbished — freshly painted, re-carpeted throughout and now featuring a brand new ensuite shower room — it’s the perfect comfortable base for exploring Belfast and beyond. Tea, coffee and milk are provided to get you settled in.

The Europa Bus Station is a 2 minute walk, with direct links to both Belfast airports. Great Victoria Street train station is steps away, and the Crown Bar, Grand Opera House and Victoria Square shopping are all within easy walking distance.

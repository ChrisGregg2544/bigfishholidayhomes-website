About the Property

The Cloisters,
Belfast

6 The Cloisters is a ground floor apartment in a private gated complex with remote access — giving you a quiet, secure base from which to explore Belfast. The name ‘Big Fish’ itself derives from the famous salmon sculpture at the Lagan Lookout, celebrating the regeneration of Belfast.

The apartment is 700 metres from Queens University and within walking distance of the world-famous Botanic Gardens. The excellent restaurants and bars of Botanic Avenue and the Ormeau Road are right on your doorstep, and Belfast city centre is just a short taxi or bus ride away.

Fully refurbished and redecorated to the highest standards, the property features fast fibre WiFi, a luxury kitchen, and on-site parking — perfect if you’re planning to travel around Northern Ireland during your stay.
